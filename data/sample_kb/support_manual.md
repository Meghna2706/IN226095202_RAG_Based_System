# CUSTOMER SUPPORT KNOWLEDGE BASE
**ShopEasy E-Commerce Platform**

## SECTION 1: RETURNS & REFUNDS
Our return policy allows customers to return items within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in their original condition with tags attached.

**To initiate a return:**
1. Log into your account at shopeasy.com
2. Navigate to Order History
3. Click "Return Item" next to the relevant order
4. Select your reason and print the prepaid return label

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

For items damaged during shipping, we offer an immediate replacement or full refund without requiring the item to be returned.

## SECTION 2: SHIPPING
- **Standard Shipping**: 5-7 business days — FREE on orders over $50
- **Express Shipping**: 2-3 business days — $9.99
- **Overnight Shipping**: Next business day — $19.99

We ship to all 50 US states and 25 international countries. International orders may take 10-21 business days and may be subject to customs duties.

Orders placed before 2 PM EST on business days ship the same day. A tracking number is emailed to you when your order ships.

## SECTION 3: ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT
- **To reset your password**: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Password reset links expire after 24 hours.
- You can update your email, shipping address, and payment methods in **Account Settings**.
- **For account deletion requests**: Contact [support@shopeasy.com](mailto:support@shopeasy.com) with your account email. Account data is permanently deleted within 30 days of the request.

## SECTION 4: PAYMENTS
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are encrypted using 256-bit SSL.

If a charge appears on your statement that you did not authorize, contact us within 60 days and we will investigate immediately.

## SECTION 5: PRODUCT WARRANTY
- **Electronics**: 1-year manufacturer warranty.
- **Clothing and accessories**: 90-day quality guarantee.
- **Furniture items**: 2 years against manufacturing defects.

**To file a warranty claim**: Email [warranty@shopeasy.com](mailto:warranty@shopeasy.com) with your order number and photos.

## SECTION 6: CONTACT & ESCALATION
- **Live Chat**: Available Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST
- **Email**: [support@shopeasy.com](mailto:support@shopeasy.com) (response within 24 hours)
- **Phone**: 1-800-SHOPEASY (available 24/7 for urgent issues)

Complex issues involving billing disputes over $500, legal matters, or account security incidents are handled by our senior support team.
